About This Dashboard

This interactive dashboard was created to provide clear, data-driven insights into the COVID-19 pandemic's global impact. Our goal is to:

  • Visualize infection trends and vaccination progress worldwide
  • Help researchers and the public understand pandemic dynamics
  • Track mortality rates and country-specific outbreaks
  • Support evidence-based decision making

Data sources: Our World in Data
